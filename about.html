About Us

History Since 2003

MAS International Co.

Since its inception in Kuwait in 2003 as an Islamic sharia- compliant entity, Mas International Co. has been thriving as one of the leading innovative real estate marketing service providers. The company excels in providing provisional real estate solution and investor / owner advisory services by promoting real estate units and products in the local and international markets.

Moreover, the company activities extend to cover other business areas, such as travel, tourism, and Haj & Umarah. Mas international Company is 100% owned by munshaat real estate projects co. and has been privileged for being the first to launch sukuk Al-intifaa (usufruct sukuk) .
